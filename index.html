About the Company

A dependable name in diesel power

RV Electricals & Engineers has spent over a decade supplying, servicing and renting diesel generators across homes, shops, factories, hospitals, telecom sites and event venues. We work with Kirloskar, Cummins, Mahindra, Ashok Leyland and Escorts, across the full 5 KVA to 1000 KVA range.

Every generator we sell or rent is backed by our own AMC and repair team — so the relationship doesn't end at delivery, it starts there.

  • Authorised dealing across 5 major generator brands
  • In-house AMC, repair and genuine spare parts team
  • New, used and rental options under one roof
